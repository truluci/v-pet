About vPet

vPet is an innovative platform designed to help you manage and care for your virtual pets. Our mission is to provide a fun and interactive experience for pet lovers everywhere. Join us and take good care of your pets!

Mission:

Our goal is to provide important information to increase awareness of the importance of our live animal friends worldwide, and we invite people who will record and monitor them to our site.

Vision:

After obtaining infinite data about all our animal friends and sharing it with the world, we will try to provide them with friends who will take care of them and not leave them homeless.
